Our Story

Crafted with
passion & fire

Born from a love of open-fire cooking and seasonal ingredients, Ember & Oak has been a cornerstone of New York's culinary landscape since 2012. Every dish on our menu is a conversation between flame and nature.

Our wood-fired kitchen uses only locally sourced, sustainably grown produce. The menu changes with the seasons, because great food follows nature's rhythm.
